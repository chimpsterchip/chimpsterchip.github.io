Hot Pot

Twin Stick Shooter x Bullet Hell

Hot Pot is a Twin Stick shooter bullet hell cross, with a quirky art style and wacky enemies. You play as the infamous pirate captain Saucy who longs to be recognized as a world class chef, however; due to her notorious reputation as a pirate captain, no culinary school will accept her. Saucy sets out on a journey to the nearby floating islands to gather all the ingredients she needs to make the best hot pot of her life so the culinary schools will finally be able to see past the pirate she is and see the chef she could

  • Production Phase: Prototype Completed
  • Release Date: June 24 2018
  • Genre: Twin Stick Shooter x Bullet Hell
  • Game Engine: Unity


Reigndeer

VR Boss Battle

Reigndeer is a 1v3 VR boss battle game where one player takes control of Rudolph, santa's own 30 foot monster complete with rocket fist, missle antlers and bombs. To challenge Rudolph there is Santa with his trusty rifle, the Elves in a sleigh and Rudolphs ex in her gingerbread armour.

  • Production Phase: Prototype Completed
  • Release Date: TBA
  • Genre: Boss Battle
  • Game Engine: Unity
